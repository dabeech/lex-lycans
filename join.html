Are you ready to start your journey? No matter which path you choose - the open hand self defense that is Shotokan Karate, or the fully armored combat that is Buhurt - you can be confident in the fact that we will do all that we can to help you become a stronger version of yourself.


Want to do both? That's a great option too! The foundations of Shotokan Karate will sharpen your skills for armored combat. A strong stance and bodily awareness will make you a force to be reckoned with in the ring.


All are welcome! Both Shotokan Karate and Buhurt are activities in which anyone can participate. Come join us for a lesson to see what we're all about.
